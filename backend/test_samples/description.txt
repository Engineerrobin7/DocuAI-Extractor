# Test Samples for DocuAI-Extractor

To test the system, please provide the following files in the `test_samples/` directory:

## PDF Samples (5)
1. **invoice.pdf**: A sample invoice with currency symbols and itemized tables.
2. **resume.pdf**: A standard professional resume for entity extraction (PERSON, ORG).
3. **report.pdf**: A long text document to test BART summarization.
4. **contract.pdf**: A legal document for entity and sentiment analysis.
5. **scanned_document.pdf**: A scanned image-based PDF for OCR testing.

## DOCX Samples (5)
1. **agreement.docx**: A sample business agreement.
2. **letter.docx**: A formal letter for entity and sentiment.
3. **proposal.docx**: A project proposal for summarization.
4. **meeting_minutes.docx**: A document with dates and participants.
5. **receipt.docx**: A document with prices and amounts.

## Image Samples (5)
1. **receipt.jpg**: A scanned receipt for OCR and money extraction.
2. **screenshot.png**: A screenshot of a text document.
3. **handwriting.jpg**: A clear handwritten note for OCR testing.
4. **id_card.png**: A sample ID card for entity extraction (PERSON).
5. **business_card.jpg**: A business card for ORG and contact info.

### Testing Instructions:
- Run the backend and frontend.
- Drag and drop these files into the upload zone.
- Verify the results in the dashboard.
